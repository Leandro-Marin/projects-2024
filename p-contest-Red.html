contest aqve romane

The challenge of this project was designing an online game for a contest by the perfume brand "Laura Biagiotti" for the launch of its new fragrances, Aqve Romane. The goal of the game was to guess the ingredients of each perfume, with the user having to drag each ingredient to the correct bottle. Three prizes were up for grabs: a shower gel, an Aqve Romane perfume, or a weekend at a spa. The project was carried out at Figmenta.
